Project: COVID-19 Analysis - Deaths, Infections, and Vaccination Impact

This project involved utilizing SQL, Excel, and Power BI to conduct a comprehensive analysis of global COVID-19 data. The primary objectives were to explore trends, assess the impact of interventions, and visualize key insights for stakeholders.

Methods Used

We employed SQL for data exploration and preparation, Excel for data organization and preprocessing, and Power BI for interactive visualization. Key techniques included:

  • Data querying and aggregation in SQL.
  • Data cleaning and formatting in Excel.
  • Creating interactive dashboards in Power BI.

Data Analysis

The analysis focused on extracting actionable insights from COVID-19 data, including:

  • Identifying countries with the highest infection rates, mortality rates, and vaccination coverage.
  • Assessing the effectiveness of public health measures in controlling the spread of the virus.
  • Evaluating the impact of vaccination campaigns on reducing COVID-19 transmission and fatalities.

Insights Gained

The analysis revealed several key insights:

  • Countries with higher vaccination rates experienced lower infection rates and mortality rates.
  • There were notable disparities in vaccination coverage between regions, highlighting the need for equitable distribution.
  • Temporal analysis showed fluctuations in COVID-19 trends, indicating the dynamic nature of the pandemic.
